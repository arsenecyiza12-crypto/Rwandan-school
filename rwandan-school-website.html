About Us

Our Story

Kigali Excellence School is a leading educational institution in Rwanda, committed to providing quality, affordable education to students from all backgrounds. Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to nurturing young minds and developing responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

We believe that every child deserves access to quality education regardless of their socioeconomic background. Our school operates on the principle that education is a fundamental right and a pathway to breaking the cycle of poverty.

In alignment with Rwanda's Vision 2050 and the national education policy, we focus on developing critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills that prepare our students for the challenges of the modern world.

Our Mission

To provide quality, accessible, and affordable education that develops well-rounded individuals with strong academic foundations, moral values, and practical skills necessary for success in higher education and the workplace.

Our Vision

To be Rwanda's leading institution for quality education, recognized for producing graduates who are academically excellent, morally upright, and equipped to drive national development and contribute to the global community.

Admissions

Admission Requirements

We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds. Here are the basic requirements for admission:

  1. Birth Certificate: Original or certified copy
  2. Previous School Report: Academic records from previous school (if applicable)
  3. Medical Report: Current health examination report
  4. Parental Information: Contact details and identification documents
  5. Entrance Assessment: Students may be required to sit for placement tests
  6. Application Form: Completed school application form
  7. School Fees: Payment of registration and first term fees

School Fees

We are committed to providing affordable quality education. Our fees are structured to be accessible to families across different income levels:

  • Nursery: RWF 200,000 - 300,000 per term
  • Primary: RWF 250,000 - 400,000 per term
  • Secondary: RWF 350,000 - 500,000 per term

Note: Scholarships and payment plans are available for qualified students. Contact our admissions office for more information.
